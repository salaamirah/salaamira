Salaamira — U.S. Shipping & Returns

Here are Salaamira’s U.S. shipping times. Please note that delivery times may vary.

Shipping

Economy Shipping: Estimated delivery within 5–7 business days.

Shipping rates will be calculated at checkout based on your location.

Processing time: All orders are shipped within 2–3 business days after being placed. Please expect possible delays during major launches. Orders placed after 3 PM EST will be processed on the next business day.

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a shipping confirmation with tracking details.

Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not, you may request a refund or store credit by following the steps below.

Return Requirements

  • Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in their original packaging with all tags attached.

How to Initiate a Return

  1. Log in to your Salaamira account on our website.
  2. Navigate to Order History and select the order you wish to return.
  3. Click Request a Return and follow the prompts to generate your return request.
  4. Once your return is approved, package your item securely and drop it off at your nearest USPS location.

Important Notes

  • Salaamira is not responsible for return shipping fees, customs charges, or lost return packages.
  • Please allow time for processing once your return has been received.
